Honey Garlic Shrimp

Ingredients

• 500g Large Shrimp (peeled/deveined)

• 3 tbsp Honey & 2 tbsp Soy Sauce

• 4 Garlic cloves, minced

• 1 tbsp Ginger, grated

• 1 tbsp Butter


Step 1: Prep and Marinate

• Whisk honey, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger in a bowl.

• Toss the shrimp in half the mixture; keep the other half for the final glaze.

Extra Details

Dividing the sauce is crucial. The portion used for marinating will be diluted by the shrimp's moisture and contains raw juices, while the "clean" half will reduce into a thick, glossy syrup in the final seconds of cooking.

Step 2: The Flash Sear

• Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high. Add shrimp in a single layer.

• Sear for 60-90 seconds per side until they turn pink and opaque. Do not overcook!

Extra Details

Shrimp cook in a heartbeat. You are looking for a "C" shape. If they curl into a tight "O," they are overdone and will be rubbery. Working in a single layer ensures they sear and caramelize rather than steaming in a crowded pan.

Step 3: Glaze and Finish

• Add the reserved sauce and a knob of butter to the pan.

• Toss for 1 minute as the sauce bubbles and clings to the shrimp. Serve immediately over rice.

Extra Details

The butter acts as an emulsifier, creating a "velvet" texture that helps the honey-soy glaze coat the smooth surface of the shrimp. A final squeeze of fresh lemon (optional) can help cut through the sweetness of the honey.
